ToughLove

ToughLove provides people harsh heart-to-heart that fit their current mood or emotional state. When the user invokes the skill for the first time, it introduces how the skill came about and how to use it. The skill is designed to lift the user's spirits with quick advice or storytelling.

As a conversation designer, I wanted the experience of the end-user to be intuitive and simple but with an emphasis on entertainment. Fortunately, I already had someone in mind to be the "voice" of ToughLove, rather than rely on Alexa's not-so-warm or anthropomorphized voice.
